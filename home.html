Maximal Coverage

Electrostatic spray systems expel electrostatically charged droplets that create a force which pulls the spray towards a target at a force 75 times that of gravity. The process ensures that the solution makes contact with all surface bacteria and viruses.

Environmentally Sound

Electrostatic spray systems reduce chemical waste, use low-toxicity chemicals, and lowers exposure to chemicals.
